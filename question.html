Mock Examination – The Rudiments of AI

Course Code: 25UNI0130 | Total Marks: 75 | Time: 5–8 PM

Section A – MCQ (6 × 1 = 6)

  1. Artificial Intelligence was developed by whom in 1956?
    (A) Norbert Wiener (B) John McCarthy & Alan Turing (C) Marvin Minsky (D) Judea Pearl)
  2. Three cognitive skills of AI programming are?
    (A) Planning, Emotion, Perception (B) Learning, Reasoning, Self-Correction (C) Vision, Speech, Motion (D) Memory, Empathy, Ethics)
  3. Which AI type does not currently exist?
    (A) Reactive Machines (B) Limited Memory (C) Theory of Mind (D) Rule-based)
  4. ChatGPT was launched on:
    (A) Nov 30, 2022 (B) Jan 20, 2025 (C) Dec 6, 2023 (D) Oct 1, 2021)
  5. Deepseek R1 was launched on:
    (A) Nov 30, 2022 (B) Jan 20, 2025 (C) Dec 6, 2023 (D) Jan 1, 2024)
  6. In the Turing Test, a machine passes when:
    (A) Beats human at chess (B) Writes original poetry (C) Judge can’t tell machine from human (D) Speaks multiple languages)

Section B – Short Answer (5 × 2 = 10)

  1. Explain how AI works (learning, reasoning, self-correction).
  2. List any four stages of AI.
  3. State two advantages of AI with examples.
  4. What is Prompt Engineering and its importance?
  5. Write any four things ChatGPT cannot do.

Section C – Long Answer (3 × 5 = 15)

  1. Explain the four types of AI with notes on current availability.
  2. Discuss advantages and disadvantages of ChatGPT.
  3. Define Metaverse and describe three real use-cases.

Section D – Assertion & Reason (2 × 3 = 6)

  1. A: AI can displace routine jobs. R: Automation enables machines to handle repetitive tasks.
    Choose A/B/C/D
  2. A: The OpenAI logo shows union of AI and human intelligence. R: It is made of two overlapping circles.
    Choose A/B/C/D

Section E – Case Study (2 × 4 = 8)

  1. Smart Traffic System infographic. (a) Two AI capabilities (b) Benefits (c) One risk.
  2. AI in Education infographic. (a) Map to AI advantages (b) One ethical concern & mitigation.

Section F – Competency Based (3 × 10 = 30)

  1. Design an AI Campus Assistant Chatbot (data, reasoning, feedback, safeguards, success metrics).
  2. Compare OpenAI, Deepseek, and Meta AI (budget, team, time, strengths, limitations, users).
  3. Propose a Metaverse-based training lab for surgeons (objectives, scenarios, tools, benefits, risks).
